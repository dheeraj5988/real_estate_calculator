03 · Before You Sign

Strategic
Pre-Buy Checklist

Non-negotiable filters before deploying capital into Indian real estate.

  • ₹
    30–40% Capital Reserve
    Have at least 30–40% of property value in liquid savings — covering down payment, stamp duty, interior fit-outs, and a 6-month EMI emergency buffer. Never enter leveraged at 5–10% equity.
  • ⚖
    A-Khata + Occupancy Certificate
    Verify municipal A-Khata status and valid OC/CC. B-Khata properties lose access to Tier-1 bank loans, face resale illiquidity, and carry demolition risk.
  • 📋
    RERA Registration Verified
    For under-construction properties, confirm RERA registration on the state portal. This protects against builder default and delivery delays.
  • 🏦
    White Transactions Only
    Never engage in cash-component deals. Sellers asking for "black" reduce paper value, limiting future resale price and LTV for refinancing.
  • 📈
    EMI ≤ 35% of Net Take-Home
    Banks enforce this, but also enforce it personally. An EMI consuming 50–60% of salary leaves no buffer for market downturns or job changes.
  • 🌐
    Growth Corridor Targeting
    Prefer tech-belt and infrastructure-growth corridors over central CBD. Better yields, higher appreciation, newer stock.
  • 🚫
    No Personal Loans for Down Payment
    Funding the down payment via personal/NBFC loans spikes your DTI ratio, causing the primary mortgage to be rejected during bank underwriting.
  • ⏳
    10+ Year Holding Horizon
    Real estate outperforms renting + investing only beyond a 10–12 year holding period in above-average appreciation markets. Short-horizon buyers rarely recoup transaction costs.
